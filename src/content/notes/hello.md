This is the first thing growing here. It's rough on purpose.

A blog made me feel like everything had to be finished before it earned a
date. A garden doesn't — a seedling is allowed to be three sentences and a
half-formed idea. So this note exists mostly to prove the pipeline works, and
to give future-me permission to plant things before they're ready.
